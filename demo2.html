Eligibility Requirements

Basic requirements to become a LIC agent

Minimum Criteria

  • Age: Minimum 18 years old
  • Education: Class 10 pass (12th/Graduation preferred)
  • Skills: Good communication and interpersonal skills
  • Network: Ability to connect with potential clients

Required Documents

  • Recent passport-size photograph
  • PAN Card copy
  • Aadhaar Card / Address proof
  • Educational certificates (10th/12th/Graduation)
  • Cancelled cheque / Bank passbook

FAQ

Do I need to pass the IRDAI exam?

Yes — pass the IRDAI exam. We provide training and mock tests to help you prepare.

Is this suitable part-time?

Absolutely — many agents start as a side income and scale up later.

How much can I earn?

Earnings depend on business. First-year commissions plus renewals create growing income over time.

Do you charge fees?

No fees for joining the agency. Any official charges (if applicable) are communicated by LIC/IRDAI only.
