FAQ

Do I need to pass the IRDAI exam?

Yes. We provide training and mock tests so you clear it with confidence.

Is this suitable part-time?

Absolutely. Many agents start alongside a job or studies and later go full-time.

How much can I earn?

Earnings depend on your business. First-year commissions + renewals create growing income over time.

Locations you onboard?

Mumbai & nearby preferred; PAN-India onboarding is possible case-to-case via online process.
